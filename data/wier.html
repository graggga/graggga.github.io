We're

Those failing lungs.
Those widening eyes.
Pretend it isn't there.

Pure oxygen.
Pure mind.
The smoke, the fibres,
the black coffee you've always drunk.

That uncontrolled temper.
Hide it from the children.
That misunderstood depression.
Hide it from yourself.

Your tinkerer life.
Your weathered skin.
Those trinkets, those gadgets, those toys.
Press that button again.

You built and destroyed your empires.
You wither and burn to dust.
No funeral. No closure.
We remain.
