I write in a number of places. Some formal, others informal. All though are subject to scrutiny of being associated with what I am expected to create.

This is where I can hide. Take refuge from association. Allow anonymous unbridled thought to flow.

This won't be the place for me to unleash my innermost offensive thoughts. Maybe in the future, If I become that way inclined. For now though, it's just whatever comes to mind.
